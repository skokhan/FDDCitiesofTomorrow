# Sustainable Urban Planning & Landscape Dataset

The Sustainable Urban Planning & Landscape Dataset is designed for research on intelligent urban design, environmental sustainability, and landscape architecture. 

It includes 3,476 rows with key urban, environmental, and socio-economic indicators.

## About this file

### Key Features

- **Urban Spatial Data:** Land use type, building density, road connectivity, public transport access.
- **Environmental Data:** Air quality index, green cover percentage, carbon footprint.
- **Socio-Economic Factors:** Population density, crime rate, income levels.
- **Sustainability & Security:** Renewable energy use, disaster risk index.
- **Target Variable:** Urban Sustainability Score (0-1) indicating a city's overall sustainability.
